Crossing The Street Jokes

See how much you know about the busy intersection used by the famous chicken.

Why did the pigeon cross the road?

  1. To deliver a message.
  2. To coo at his mate.
  3. It was the chicken's day off.
  4. To fly on a statue's head.

Why did the turkey cross the road?

  1. It was almost Thanksgiving and he was hoping people would think he was a chicken.
  2. He was moulting and he wanted to drop the feathers off on the other side.
  3. He was escaping from Wile E. Coyote.
  4. He was chasing a grasshopper.

Why did the chewing gum cross the road?

  1. Someone spit it across the road.
  2. Some ants carried it across.
  3. It was blown over by the wind.
  4. It was stuck to the chicken's foot.

Why did the little boy cross the playground?

  1. To talk to a little girl.
  2. To get to the other slide.
  3. To play tetherball.
  4. To eat a bug.

Why did the dolphin cross the ocean?

  1. To get to the other tide.
  2. For a vacation.
  3. To chase a cruise ship.
  4. To eat a sardine.
